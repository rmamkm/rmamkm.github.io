Decisive Battles the Civil War

Class A battles of 1861 to 1865

The Civil War included 384 battles between the Union and Confederate armies. In 1990, the US Goverment established the Civil War Sites Advisory Commission to aide in the preservation of battlefields. This commission released a ranking system to identify the significance of battles on the outcome of the Civil War. There are 45 "Class A" battles which are described by the CWSAC as having the greatest impact on the ultimate outcome of the war. Below are the most decisive battles of 1861 to 1865.
